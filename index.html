EuroSys 2024
Athens
April 22nd-25th 2024

Overview

Welcome to the 19th edition of EuroSys!

EuroSys is a premier conference on various aspects of systems software research and development, including its ramifications for hardware and applications. Topics of interest for the conference include: operating systems, database systems, real-time systems, networked systems, storage systems, middleware, distributed, parallel, and embedded computing systems.

EuroSys 2024 will be a four-day event. The first day is devoted to workshops, to allow junior and senior researchers to interact in a friendly environment on cutting-edge ideas. The other three days are dedicated to the main conference track.

The 19th edition of EuroSys will follow the pattern established by the previous EuroSys conferences, by seeking high-quality papers on all aspects of computer systems.
